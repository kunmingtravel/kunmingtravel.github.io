Are There International Restaurants in Kunming? Recommended Spots to Visit

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Kunming, known as the "Spring City" of China for its mild climate year-round, is a bustling metropolis in the Yunnan Province. Over the past few decades, Kunming has evolved into an increasingly international city, attracting visitors from around the world due to its rich cultural heritage, scenic beauty, and vibrant food scene. As the city grows, so does its culinary diversity. International cuisine has found a home in Kunming, making it a great destination for those seeking flavors from across the globe.

Whether you’re a tourist visiting the city or an expatriate residing in Kunming, you might be interested in exploring its international restaurant offerings. From Western-style cafes to Southeast Asian dishes and even European fine dining, Kunming offers a broad spectrum of international cuisine. In this article, we’ll guide you through some of the most recommended international restaurants in the city.

1. The Greenery Café

Located in the heart of Kunming, The Greenery Café is one of the most popular international spots in the city. It is especially known for its casual and cozy atmosphere, where you can enjoy a relaxing meal with friends or family. This café offers a range of Western dishes, including classic American breakfasts, sandwiches, burgers, and fresh salads. The Greenery Café is a favorite among expats, and the menu has been tailored to meet the needs of both local and international guests.

One of the standout dishes at The Greenery Café is their homemade burger, served with crispy fries and a selection of sauces. They also have a variety of smoothies and freshly squeezed juices, perfect for a refreshing drink after a warm day in the city. If you’re craving something sweet, their cakes and pastries are a delightful treat.

The Greenery Café also provides a great selection of vegetarian options, making it an inclusive choice for people with dietary restrictions. The ambiance is relaxed, with indoor and outdoor seating available. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to enjoy a cup of coffee or a hearty meal, The Greenery Café offers both.

2. Ali Baba Kunming

For those who crave Middle Eastern flavors, Ali Baba Kunming is a fantastic option. This international restaurant offers an authentic taste of Middle Eastern cuisine, featuring traditional dishes like hummus, falafel, shawarma, and various kebabs. The restaurant is well-loved by both locals and foreigners who are fond of the aromatic spices and hearty dishes typical of the Middle Eastern region.

What sets Ali Baba Kunming apart is the authentic experience it provides. The dishes are rich in flavor and generously portioned, offering great value for money. The shawarma is especially popular, with the seasoned meat cooked to perfection and served with warm flatbreads and tangy sauces. The hummus and tabbouleh are also highly recommended as starters.

The restaurant’s cozy atmosphere and friendly service make it an ideal spot for both intimate dinners and group meals. Ali Baba Kunming is also known for its vegetarian-friendly options, such as their falafel platter, and is a great choice for anyone seeking a taste of the Middle East.

3. La Table de Joël Robuchon

For those looking for a truly high-end dining experience, La Table de Joël Robuchon in Kunming is the place to be. Located inside the Intercontinental Kunming, this restaurant offers French haute cuisine that is renowned for its attention to detail, exceptional flavors, and artistic presentation.

La Table de Joël Robuchon provides a Michelin-star dining experience, where you can indulge in the finest French delicacies. The menu features a variety of exquisite dishes, including lobster bisque, foie gras, and a selection of beautifully prepared meats, all complemented by an extensive wine list. The restaurant’s signature dish, the Robuchon mashed potatoes, is a must-try and is often considered one of the best mashed potatoes in the world.

This restaurant is perfect for special occasions or anyone who appreciates the art of French cuisine. The ambiance is elegant, with a sophisticated atmosphere that provides the ideal setting for a memorable meal. If you are a food lover and wish to experience world-class French dining in Kunming, La Table de Joël Robuchon is a top recommendation.

4. Dali Thai

Kunming’s proximity to Southeast Asia makes it a prime location for excellent Thai food. Dali Thai is one of the top Thai restaurants in Kunming, offering an authentic experience with a wide range of dishes. The restaurant serves traditional Thai cuisine, including dishes such as pad Thai, green curry, tom yum soup, and mango sticky rice.

The flavors at Dali Thai are bold, with plenty of spices and herbs, making the food both flavorful and aromatic. The green curry is a crowd favorite, prepared with a perfect balance of spicy and creamy coconut flavors. For those who enjoy seafood, the tom yum soup is another popular choice, with its tangy and spicy broth paired with fresh shrimp.

Dali Thai also provides an inviting ambiance, with wooden furniture and traditional Thai decorations that add to the overall cultural experience. Whether you’re looking for a quick meal or a leisurely dinner, Dali Thai is an excellent spot to explore the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine in Kunming.

5. Mango Tree Kunming

If you’re in the mood for Southeast Asian fusion cuisine, Mango Tree is a must-try restaurant. Known for its modern interpretation of traditional Thai and Vietnamese dishes, Mango Tree brings a unique twist to Southeast Asian cuisine by combining fresh ingredients and bold flavors in innovative ways.

The restaurant is famous for its Thai-style curries, Vietnamese pho, and fresh spring rolls. A standout dish at Mango Tree is their yellow curry, which is rich in coconut milk and spiced to perfection. The pho, a Vietnamese noodle soup, is another crowd favorite, known for its aromatic broth and tender beef slices.

Mango Tree is also well-loved for its contemporary ambiance, which blends a relaxed, open-air dining experience with modern design. If you're looking for something fresh and new with an international flair, Mango Tree Kunming offers a fantastic culinary journey through Southeast Asia.

6. The Blue Lotus

For those who love Indian cuisine, The Blue Lotus in Kunming is an excellent choice. Located in the city center, this restaurant offers a wide array of traditional Indian dishes, including butter chicken, naan, dal makhani, and biryani. Known for its rich, flavorful, and aromatic curries, The Blue Lotus is a favorite spot for anyone craving a taste of India in Kunming.

The butter chicken is a standout dish at The Blue Lotus, with its creamy, mildly spiced sauce that perfectly complements the tender chicken. The biryani, made with fragrant basmati rice and aromatic spices, is another dish worth trying. You can also enjoy a variety of vegetarian options, such as paneer tikka and aloo gobi, making it a great place for both vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.

The ambiance at The Blue Lotus is casual yet elegant, with Indian-inspired decor and a cozy atmosphere that makes it an ideal place to enjoy a flavorful Indian meal with friends or family.

7. The Italian Restaurant

If you're craving classic Italian cuisine, Kunming has several great spots to try, but The Italian Restaurant stands out as one of the most popular choices. Located in the city center, this restaurant serves up authentic Italian dishes like pasta, pizza, risotto, and lasagna.

Their pizzas are hand-tossed and baked in a wood-fired oven, giving them a deliciously crispy crust. The pasta is freshly made, and you can choose from a variety of sauces, including the rich and creamy carbonara or the tangy pomodoro. For dessert, their tiramisu is a must-try, as it is light, creamy, and perfectly sweetened.

The Italian Restaurant offers a welcoming atmosphere with warm lighting and comfortable seating, making it a great place for both casual and romantic dinners. If you’re in the mood for authentic Italian comfort food, this restaurant will not disappoint.

8. The Canteen

If you’re a fan of Western-style fast food with a healthy twist, The Canteen is a great place to check out in Kunming. Offering a variety of salads, smoothies, wraps, and sandwiches, this restaurant focuses on providing quick, nutritious meals for busy travelers and locals.

The wraps and sandwiches are particularly popular, filled with fresh vegetables, lean meats, and flavorful sauces. The Canteen also offers a selection of healthy juices and smoothies that are perfect for a light and refreshing meal.

The Canteen has a trendy, modern vibe, making it a great spot for young professionals and anyone looking for a healthy alternative to traditional fast food. It’s the perfect place to grab a quick and nutritious meal while exploring Kunming.

9. Nico's Café

A local favorite for expats and tourists alike, Nico’s Café serves an array of international dishes, from Italian pasta to French pastries. Known for its welcoming and relaxed atmosphere, this café offers both Western and Asian options, ensuring there's something for everyone. You can enjoy a delicious cup of coffee while choosing from their selection of homemade sandwiches or freshly baked croissants.

Their brunch menu is highly praised, featuring a variety of dishes such as eggs benedict, waffles, and hearty omelettes. Nico’s Café also serves a variety of desserts, with the chocolate cake and cheesecake being popular choices among regulars.

The café’s interior is cozy and stylish, making it a great place for catching up with friends or enjoying a quiet meal alone. If you're in Kunming and craving a relaxed and tasty meal with a touch of international flair, Nico’s Café is a top recommendation.

Kunming’s food scene is diverse and welcoming, offering a wide range of international dining options that cater to various tastes and preferences. Whether you’re in the mood for a taste of Europe, the Middle East, or Southeast Asia, Kunming has something to satisfy every craving. The restaurants listed above represent just a few of the many international dining experiences available in this vibrant city. With new options constantly emerging, Kunming continues to develop as a culinary destination that offers delicious food from all corners of the globe.
