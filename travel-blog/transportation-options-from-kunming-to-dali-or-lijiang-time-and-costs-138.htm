Transportation Options from Kunming to Dali or Lijiang: Time and Costs

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province, is a major gateway to two of the region's most famous tourist destinations: Dali and Lijiang. Each of these cities offers unique charm, historical significance, and natural beauty. If you're planning a trip from Kunming to Dali or Lijiang, understanding the various transportation options, their durations, and costs will help you make the most efficient choice.

Traveling from Kunming to Dali

By High-Speed Train

The high-speed train is one of the most convenient and efficient ways to travel from Kunming to Dali. The trains run frequently and are known for their comfort and punctuality.

  • Duration: Approximately 2 to 2.5 hours.
  • Cost:
    • Second Class Seat: Around ¥145.
    • First Class Seat: Around ¥230.
    • Business Class Seat: Around ¥450.

Benefits of High-Speed Train

  • Speed and Comfort: The trains are modern, spacious, and equipped with air conditioning.
  • Scenic Views: The route passes through picturesque landscapes, offering glimpses of Yunnan's natural beauty.
  • Convenience: Kunming South Railway Station and Dali Railway Station are well-connected to city centers via public transportation.

By Long-Distance Bus

If you prefer road travel, long-distance buses are an alternative. They operate daily between Kunming and Dali and cater to budget travelers.

  • Duration: Around 5 to 6 hours, depending on traffic.
  • Cost: Approximately ¥120 to ¥150.

Pros and Cons of Bus Travel

  • Advantages: Affordable and frequent schedules.
  • Disadvantages: Longer travel time compared to trains, and the comfort level varies based on the bus company.

By Air

Flights from Kunming to Dali are available but less common due to the short distance and the efficiency of high-speed trains.

  • Duration: 50 minutes (flight time). Add 1-2 hours for check-in and security.
  • Cost: Around ¥400 to ¥800, depending on the airline and booking time.

Key Considerations

  • Flights are only advisable if you’re on a tight schedule. Otherwise, the high-speed train offers similar travel times when considering check-in and security processes at the airport.

By Private Car or Taxi

For those who value flexibility, renting a private car or taking a taxi can be a viable option.

  • Duration: 4 to 5 hours.
  • Cost: Around ¥1,000 to ¥1,500 for a private car rental or taxi service.

Why Choose This Option?

  • Ideal for groups or families who want the freedom to stop and explore along the way.

Traveling from Kunming to Lijiang

By High-Speed Train

High-speed trains also operate between Kunming and Lijiang, offering a convenient way to reach this picturesque city.

  • Duration: Approximately 3 to 3.5 hours.
  • Cost:
    • Second Class Seat: Around ¥200.
    • First Class Seat: Around ¥320.
    • Business Class Seat: Around ¥600.

Highlights of This Option

  • Efficiency: Fast and reliable with frequent departures.
  • Comfort: Spacious seats and modern amenities.
  • Scenery: The journey offers breathtaking views of mountains and valleys.

By Long-Distance Bus

For those looking to save money, long-distance buses are another option.

  • Duration: Around 8 to 9 hours.
  • Cost: Approximately ¥150 to ¥180.

What to Expect

  • Comfort: Varies depending on the bus type. Sleeper buses may be available for overnight journeys.
  • Stops: Buses may stop at various towns along the way, extending travel time.

By Air

Flights between Kunming and Lijiang are popular for travelers on tight schedules.

  • Duration: 1 hour (flight time). Add 1-2 hours for airport procedures.
  • Cost: Around ¥500 to ¥1,000, depending on the season and booking time.

Benefits of Flying

  • Time-Saving: Ideal for those who want to minimize travel time.
  • Convenience: Multiple flights are available daily from Kunming Changshui International Airport to Lijiang Sanyi International Airport.

By Private Car or Taxi

For ultimate flexibility, renting a private car or hiring a taxi can be a good choice.

  • Duration: Around 6 to 7 hours.
  • Cost: ¥1,500 to ¥2,000 for a private car rental or taxi service.

Advantages of Private Transport

  • Freedom: Allows stops at scenic spots or attractions along the way, such as Shilin (Stone Forest) or Dali if combining trips.
  • Comfort: Personalized travel experience without the hassle of public transport.

Combined Kunming-Dali-Lijiang Itinerary

For travelers planning to visit both Dali and Lijiang, the following itinerary is recommended:

  1. Kunming to Dali: Take a high-speed train (2-2.5 hours).
  2. Stay in Dali: Spend 1-2 days exploring attractions such as Erhai Lake, the Three Pagodas, and Dali Ancient Town.
  3. Dali to Lijiang: Travel by high-speed train (1.5-2 hours) or private car (2.5-3 hours).
  4. Stay in Lijiang: Spend 2-3 days enjoying highlights like Lijiang Ancient Town, Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, and Shuhe Ancient Town.

Final Thoughts

Traveling from Kunming to Dali or Lijiang offers a range of options to suit different preferences, budgets, and schedules. Whether you choose the efficiency of high-speed trains, the affordability of buses, or the flexibility of private cars, Yunnan’s incredible landscapes and vibrant cultures make the journey just as memorable as the destinations themselves.
