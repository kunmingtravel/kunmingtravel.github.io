Recommended Restaurants Around Dianchi Lake

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Dianchi Lake, located in Kunming, is not only a beautiful natural attraction but also a hub for experiencing local and international cuisine. Whether you are looking for authentic Yunnan flavors, fresh seafood, or a cozy café with lake views, the area surrounding Dianchi Lake offers a range of dining options. Here’s a guide to some of the best restaurants to check out near Dianchi Lake.

Dianchi 1910 Restaurant

Dianchi 1910 is a well-known restaurant offering a luxurious dining experience with a focus on Yunnan cuisine.

Highlights

  • Signature Dishes: Try the famous Crossing the Bridge Rice Noodles, a dish deeply rooted in Yunnan’s culinary tradition. Other specialties include roasted mushrooms and spicy chicken.
  • Ambiance: The restaurant boasts elegant interiors and lake views, making it ideal for special occasions.
  • Location: Conveniently located near the lake, it’s a great spot to unwind after exploring Dianchi’s attractions.

Shiping Huiguan

Shiping Huiguan is a favorite among locals and tourists for its authentic Yunnan dishes and traditional architecture.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shiping Tofu: Known for its silky texture and rich flavors, this dish is a must.
  • Wild Mushroom Hotpot: Yunnan is famous for its variety of mushrooms, and this hotpot showcases their earthy flavors.
  • Pu’er Tea Pairing: Complement your meal with a cup of local Pu’er tea for a truly regional experience.

Haidilao Hotpot

For those craving hotpot, Haidilao is a popular chain that delivers a consistent and enjoyable dining experience.

Why Choose Haidilao?

  • Customizable Options: Choose from a variety of broths, including spicy, tomato, and mushroom-based soups.
  • Fresh Ingredients: From sliced meats to seafood and vegetables, the quality is impeccable.
  • Entertainment: The restaurant is known for its noodle-making performances and excellent service.

Dian Lake International Seafood Restaurant

As the name suggests, this restaurant specializes in fresh seafood dishes with a touch of Yunnan flavors.

Notable Dishes

  • Grilled Fish: Choose from a variety of locally caught fish, grilled to perfection with flavorful spices.
  • Shrimp Dishes: Try the garlic-steamed shrimp for a fragrant and savory option.
  • Seafood Platter: Perfect for sharing, the platter includes crab, clams, and squid.

Yunnan Taste Garden

This restaurant is an excellent choice for those seeking a more casual yet authentic dining experience.

What to Eat

  • Yunnan-Style Barbecue: Grilled meats and vegetables seasoned with local spices are a favorite.
  • Rice Noodles: Their version of Crossing the Bridge Rice Noodles is flavorful and satisfying.
  • Dried Beef with Chili: A spicy and savory dish that pairs well with a cold beer.

Baiyun Cuisine

Located near the Western Hills Scenic Area, Baiyun Cuisine combines scenic views with high-quality food.

Menu Highlights

  • Steamed Fish: Prepared with local herbs and spices, the fish is tender and aromatic.
  • Vegetable Dishes: Seasonal greens cooked with Yunnan’s signature ingredients like ham and garlic.
  • Desserts: Try the rose flower cakes for a sweet and floral end to your meal.

Laofangzi

Laofangzi, meaning “Old House,” is a charming restaurant housed in a traditional building.

Why It’s Unique

  • Historical Setting: The restaurant’s décor reflects Yunnan’s cultural heritage.
  • Mushroom Dishes: Yunnan’s reputation as the mushroom capital shines through in their diverse offerings.
  • Comfort Food: From stews to dumplings, the menu is filled with hearty, comforting options.

Western Café Options

If you’re in the mood for something different, there are also several cafés around Dianchi Lake offering Western cuisine and stunning views.

Examples

  • Lakeview Café: Known for its coffee, pastries, and tranquil setting by the water.
  • French Bistro: Offers classics like croissants, quiche, and French onion soup.
  • Italian Restaurant: Pizza and pasta lovers will enjoy this cozy spot near the lake.

Vegetarian-Friendly Restaurants

For vegetarian visitors, there are several restaurants around Dianchi Lake that cater to plant-based diets.

Recommendations

  • Green Lotus Vegetarian: This restaurant specializes in innovative plant-based versions of traditional Yunnan dishes.
  • Temple Cuisine: Located near temples, these eateries offer simple yet flavorful vegetarian fare.
  • Salad Bars: Several cafés in the area offer fresh salads and light options for health-conscious diners.

Street Food Around Dianchi Lake

If you prefer a more casual dining experience, explore the street food options near the lake.

Popular Snacks

  • Fried Potatoes: Topped with chili powder and herbs, these are a crunchy and spicy delight.
  • Glutinous Rice Cakes: A sweet treat often filled with sesame or red bean paste.
  • Grilled Skewers: From meat to vegetables, these are a quick and satisfying option.

Tips for Dining Around Dianchi Lake

  1. Reservations: Popular restaurants can get crowded, especially during weekends and holidays. Make a reservation in advance to secure your spot.
  2. Local Specialties: Don’t miss out on Yunnan’s signature dishes, including rice noodles, mushrooms, and wild herbs.
  3. Language Barrier: While many restaurants have English menus, learning a few food-related phrases in Mandarin or using a translation app can enhance your experience.
  4. Scenic Views: Opt for outdoor seating where available to enjoy the beauty of Dianchi Lake while dining.
  5. Try Everything: From fine dining to street food, the variety of flavors around Dianchi Lake ensures there’s something for everyone.

Dining around Dianchi Lake is a culinary journey that complements its natural beauty. With so many options to explore, your visit to this stunning destination will be as satisfying for your taste buds as it is for your soul.
